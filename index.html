About Our Learning Website

Our learning website is designed specifically for dyslexic children, providing them with a supportive and inclusive environment to enhance their learning abilities. With our advanced text to speech converter, children can listen to the content instead of struggling with reading. Our text recognition technology helps them improve their reading skills by recognizing and highlighting words as they read along.
